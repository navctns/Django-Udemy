Who we are?

Wineyard Cafe is one of North Americans favorite hangout place.The most we care about is our guests. We try hard to provide the best experience to them always.All our coffees are sourced from thousands of small planters from India and South Africa. These many years of trade with them made Us one of the perfect coffee making partners in the world. We opened our first Cafe in Naramata British Columbia as a small garage cafe beside our vineyards by Mr and Mrs Chris Lee. Mr Chris was a Motor Cycle mechanic. They aimed those summer riders passing by. It was a one stop destination on the highway for riders to relax , repair and condition their Bikes. They sold Wines, coffees and donuts.Gradually youth and elders took us to their heart thats where we started growing and still continued to be the favorite place to hangout. Now we have our Cafes 24/7 Opened in 45 North American cities.
